Silva North provides practical, expert and independent advice on woodlands, forestry and trees in the built environment.

Arboricultural Consultancy

Arboricultural survey work carried out and professional advice are provided to help landowners meet their responsibilities for tree safety, while ensuring the ecological, amenity and long-term value of trees and urban woodlands is properly recognised. Services include tree safety surveys, BS5837 reports and management plans for individual trees of value.

Forestry Management

Silva North provides the full range of forestry management services, from practical woodland operations to management planning. All necessary permissions and licences can be managed, along with the identification and securing of grant funding where available
